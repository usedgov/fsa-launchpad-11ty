## PIV Card Access Guide
1.	**Complete Initial Forms:** All candidates must fill out the reciprocity check form. The staff package should also include the RRED form, PDR, Fair Credit Release form, and the optional 306 form.
2.	**Reciprocity Clearance:** Candidates who already have clearance from a previous position may submit an ORF via ICAM. The ORF is needed for PIV card issuance, GFE access, and an ed.gov email account.
3.	**Background Clearance (No Reciprocity):** If a candidate does not have reciprocity, they must be submitted and initiated in NBIS for background clearance. Attachments required are the PDR, Fair Credit form, 306 form, and the cybersecurity certification.
4.	**New Fingerprints and Photo (Reciprocity):** Candidates with reciprocity who require updated fingerprints or a photo should submit an ORF via ICAM to process these items.
5.	**Upload Documentation:** For each candidate, the PDR must be uploaded to ICAM.
6.	**Cybersecurity Training:** Once the ORF is initiated, candidates will receive an email from Security Management to complete their Cybersecurity training certification.
7.	**PIV Card Notification:** After the Cybersecurity certificate is submitted and cleared, the system will automatically send an email from HSPD12Admin@usaccess.gsa.gov with instructions for PIV card pickup.
8.	**GFE Submission:** The GFE submission form must be completed by FSA internal staff, following communication from ICAM.
9.	**Final Approval:** Once the candidate is approved and adjudicated, they will receive a Pre-appointment notification and their PHN#.
